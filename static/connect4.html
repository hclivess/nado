Why nobody can cheat

Every move is a signed transaction bound to its exact turn number — replays and stale retries bounce off. The contract enforces whose turn it is, drops the disc by gravity itself, and detects four-in-a-row on-chain, paying the winner in the same instant. A full board refunds both stakes automatically. If your opponent walks away, the move clock (~30 min) lets anyone refund the game — stalling only ever voids, never wins.
