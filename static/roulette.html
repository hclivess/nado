NADO Roulette

A provably-fair, peer-banked multiplayer European roulette on the NADO execution layer. A table is one shared wheel: the bank opens it, everyone bets during the window, then one spin — HASH(bankSecret ‖ tableId) mod 37 — decides every seat. A win pays the true 36 ÷ numbers-covered (2.70% single-zero edge). Post-quantum wallet, no install.

Provably fair — no cheating

One shared wheel per table, enforced on-chain:
• The number is locked before any bet. The bank commits HASH(secret) when it opens the table, so the winning number is fixed and hidden before betting starts — the bank can't change it or aim it at the bets, and bettors bet blind. When the window closes the bank reveals the secret and the one number is derived for everyone.
• Every seat is independent. You only sign once — your bet. A no-show never stalls the table, and one reveal settles all seats. A win pays the true 36 ÷ numbers-covered.
• The bank can't run. If the bank never spins, after the deadline every seat claims its maximum win — so stalling always costs the bank more than spinning. The bank escrows enough to cover every seat's max at once.
• House wins are withdrawable. Each bank escrows only its own table's bankroll and reclaims it (± results) with one close — no shared pool, no trust.

Runs on NADO · contract calls ride L1 as ordered blobs, apply at finality (a few minutes). See the contract.
