Provably fair — no hidden minefield

There is no server holding a secret mine layout that could be rigged. When you reveal tiles, the bet binds to two future L1 block hashes; each tile's draw is HASH(blockhash(H) ‖ blockhash(H+1) ‖ seat ‖ n) mod tilesLeft — a mine iff it falls below your mine count. Those hashes don't exist yet when you commit, so nobody — not you, not the bank, not the block producer — can know or steer the result (two blocks are mixed so no single producer can grind it). Every safe tile multiplies your payout by tilesLeft·99 / ((tilesLeft−mines)·100) — the exact fair odds with a flat 1% edge — and the contract enforces the math and pays your cash-out automatically.
