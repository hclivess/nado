Digital pets that live on the chain — true NFTs you own, name, feed, train, battle and trade. Adopt an egg for 1 NADO; a future block hash nobody can predict decides which of 100 animals hatches — dogs, cats, turtles, fish, bugs, octopuses, monkeys… 70 common, 25 rare and 5 legendary (Dragon, Phoenix, Kraken, Unicorn, Star Whale), with 10 stats locked at hatch. Pets eat real NADO to stay alive — stop feeding and they die (a full belly lasts 30 days). Train them (rarer = easier), then challenge other pets to turn-based duels for stakes where every stat fights — the winner takes the pot and claims the losing pet, though the loser has a small 10% chance to die; both fighters are exhausted for 6 hours after. Sell and buy pets on the built-in marketplace. Everything — genes, species, coats, meals, training rolls, fights, sales — is decided and recorded on-chain.

Challenge this pet

A turn-based duel decided by a future block hash where every stat fights: 💪 damage · 🤸 dodge · ❤️ HP · 🧠 accuracy · 🦉 mitigation · ✨ intimidation · 🤝 regen · 🍀 crits · ⚡ turn share · 🍖 bulk — Monte-Carlo balanced so power is a true score (equal power ≈ 50/50, any +12 stat points ≈ +4-7% win). The defender's owner must accept. Winner takes both stakes and claims the losing pet — but the loser has a small 10% chance to die instead. Both fighters are then exhausted for 6 hours — no back-to-back battles.

The price goes straight from your exec balance to the current owner; the pet is yours the moment it confirms — genes, level, trained stats, hunger and all.

Transfer moves the pet to another wallet forever — it's a real NFT; the new owner feeds it (or doesn't…). Selling lists it on the marketplace below at your ask.

Adopt an egg — burn 1 NADO

Eggs are bought by burning NADO — the 1 NADO fee is destroyed forever (sent to a dead key no wallet controls), not paid to anyone. Your egg's animal is decided by a block hash that doesn't exist yet when you sign — not by us, not by you: one of 100 animals, each an exact 1% roll — 70 common, 25 rare, 5 legendary. It hatches fed for 30 days; after that it eats real NADO (burned too — about 0.1 NADO/day for an average appetite, belly holds up to 30 days) and dies if you stop. You can own as many as you can feed.

How it's provably fair

A pet's gene is HASH(blockhash(b) ‖ blockhash(b+1) ‖ petId) where block b is minted after you sign — nobody (including us) can pick or predict it. The gene fixes the animal (gene % 100 picks one of the 100 species), its rarity tier, coat and all 10 base stats forever. Training and battles bind to future block hashes the same way; the contract recomputes every roll on-chain, so the result you see is the result everyone's node computes. Adoption, food and training fees are burned — paid to a dead key no wallet can ever control — so playing shrinks the supply; there is no house wallet. Ownership is a plain on-chain record: transfer it and the pet is theirs, key and kibble.
