Why nobody can cheat

Every move is a signed transaction bound to its exact turn number — replays and stale retries bounce off. The contract enforces whose turn it is, that the cell is free, and detects three-in-a-row itself, paying the winner in the same instant. A full board refunds both stakes automatically. If your opponent walks away, the move clock (~30 min) lets anyone refund the game — stalling only ever voids, never wins.
