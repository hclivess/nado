How the wager is safe & fair

The contract is a pure wager escrow + move log — the full rules are refereed in your browser, and every move lands on-chain in order.
• Shuffles: each move pins a future block hash as its randomness — signed before the block exists, so neither player (nor the node) can rig a deck, and both browsers re-derive identical decks.
• Decisive game: the loser concedes and the winner is paid. Draw: both agree — stakes refund.
• Stall or dispute: after the move deadline either player claims a refund. The only outcomes are concede / agree / refund — no one can ever be robbed; at worst a wager voids. The contract is upgradable.
