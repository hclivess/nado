Provably fair — the contract deals & ranks

Every ~2 minutes the table deals. Each hand's 5 cards are HASH(blockhash(H) ‖ blockhash(H+1) ‖ seat ‖ i) mod 52 — those block hashes don't exist yet when you deal in, so nobody can know or steer your cards (two blocks are mixed vs a producer grind). The contract itself ranks the hand on-chain and pays the paytable multiplier, so there's no trusted evaluator and no secret. Once your deal block is final, anyone can settle your seat; a win pays automatically. Cards are drawn independently (no shuffle fits the VM), so the paytable is tuned to a ~3.5% house edge. Losing stakes fold into the bankroll.
