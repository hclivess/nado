Provably fair & safe

Both players commit a secret before either reveals, so neither can pick their hand. When both reveal, both browsers derive the same shuffled deck from HASH(secret₀‖secret₁) and deal 5 cards each — a perft-style verified evaluator ranks them identically for both of you. The pot settles by concede (loser pays the winner), agree a split (tie → refund), or a refund timeout — so a stall or dispute can only refund, never mis-pay.
