NADO Hamster Racing

🎮 All games

Six hamsters, one shared pot, no house. When a race starts, each hamster's speed gene is fixed by a future block hash and shown to everyone — that's the form you bet on. Betting stays open for a window, then the next few block hashes decide the run: the hamster that covers the most ground wins the whole pot, split among its backers (parimutuel). Genes are public before betting closes; the run is unknown until after — so nobody can cheat. Post-quantum wallet, no install.

🏆 Daily Derby — free, provably fair, faucet-rewarded

A fresh eight-race slate every day, unique to your wallet. No stake — read the form, pick your winners, and post your score. The top finishers each day are paid automatically from the NADO faucet. Every score is replay-verified on-chain, so nobody can fake the board.

🏦 Be the bank

Post a bankroll and quote a price for each hamster. Punters can then back one instantly at your price — nobody waits for a crowd. You keep every losing stake and pay the winners from your roll, so a good book earns and a bad one pays. The contract holds your roll and never lets you take a bet you could not cover.
