Provably fair — the dealer is an algorithm

Your cards bind to two future L1 block hashes the moment you bet or hit — card = HASH(blockhash(H) ‖ blockhash(H+1) ‖ hand ‖ n) mod 52 — and the dealer's hole card and hits are drawn the same way from blocks bound only after you stand. Nobody can peek, stack the deck, or grind the result (two blocks are mixed). The dealer must stand on 17 — a fixed strategy enforced by the contract, which also stores every card on-chain and pays wins (2×), pushes (refund) and naturals (5:2) automatically. Cards draw from an infinite shoe (each card independent), the one provably fair way to deal without a dealer.
