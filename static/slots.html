How it's provably fair

A spin that lands in block B is decided by HASH(blockhash(B+2) ‖ blockhash(B+3) ‖ spinId) — two blocks that don't exist yet when you sign, mixed so no single producer can steer them. Three stops (0–63) map to weighted reels, and the contract recomputes the exact same stops and pays the published paytable on-chain. The bank commits a full 150× cover for every open spin, so the machine can never welsh. No house — machines are owned and banked by players.
