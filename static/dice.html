Provably fair — no cheating

Your roll is fixed before you bet. The bank commits HASH(secret) when it opens the table, so every roll is locked and hidden before betting — the bank can't change it or aim it, and you bet blind. When the window closes the bank reveals the secret and each seat's roll is derived as HASH(secret ‖ seatId) mod 100 — your own independent throw. A win pays 99 ÷ target (1% edge). If the bank never rolls, after the deadline every seat claims its max win, so stalling always costs the bank.
