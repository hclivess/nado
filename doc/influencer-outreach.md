Outreach targets for the NADO relaunch. Built around what NADO actually is: a **fair-launch
(no premine), self-minable, privacy + post-quantum** coin from the Bismuth lineage. Positioning

crowd, not mainstream pump influencers.

## Why this list is shaped this way

- **Timing is the hook.** Privacy coins are having a 2026 moment (Monero ~$500 ATH, FCMP++ shipped
  May 2026), and Google's March 2026 paper moved the quantum-threat-to-ECDSA from theoretical to
  near-term. NADO sits at the intersection both narratives point to: **privacy _and_
  post-quantum**. Lead with that, not "new coin go up."
- **Mainstream pump influencers are misaligned.** They want premined bags to dump; NADO has no
  premine and no marketing budget, so they're pay-to-play and off-narrative. Skip them.
- **The real audience is the fair-launch / mining / cypherpunk segment** — the same OG Crypto
  Twitter crowd that covered Bismuth in 2017-18 (notsofast literally covered Bismuth). These are
  genesis miners, not just promoters.

## The pitch (fits every tier)

> Fair-launch (no premine), self-minable, privacy via **transparent post-quantum STARKs** — no
> trusted setup, quantum-safe by construction. Riding the exact privacy + PQ convergence everyone's
> watching in 2026.
