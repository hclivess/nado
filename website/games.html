Provably fair — by construction

Every game is an ordinary on-chain contract on NADO's execution layer (no special server, no house account). Outcomes come from future L1 block hashes that don't exist yet while betting is open — no player, no host, no block producer can know or steer a result (Hold'em adds commit-reveal so your hole cards stay yours alone).
