NADO Games

Provably-fair games on the NADO execution layer. Sign in with your post-quantum wallet, stake real NADO, and win — the outcome is enforced on-chain by commit-reveal, so nobody can cheat. No install, no house account.

Provably fair — by construction

Every game is an ordinary on-chain contract on NADO's execution layer (no special server, no house account). Outcomes come from commit-reveal: players commit a hash of a secret before anyone reveals, so no one can bias the result, and payouts are escrowed and settled by the contract itself — nothing is minted or lost. Your key never leaves your NADO wallet; each game only bounces you there to sign. See how the contracts work →
