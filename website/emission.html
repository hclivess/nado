The model. Reward per block = BASE_SUBSIDY · m(r), with BASE=0.1 NADO and m(r)=0.15+0.85·e−4r — the more the network bonds, the less it mints. There is no fee-weighted term and no ceiling: fees are destroyed, so net = minted − fees. Since m ≤ 1, 0.1 NADO is the max emission/block and ≈0.0166 the perpetual tail (~52,000 NADO/yr at 10s blocks, forever — production never dies, no hard cap, no security cliff).
